In BoltBreak you will take on 100s of SuperPowered Enemies alone as you fight in the Super Arena, gifted the powers of the gods it is up to you to decide how to defeat the villians and survive the arena. Will you slow time and punch the villains to death at superspeed or will you create a sonic clap that will decimate the villains where they stand, the choice is yours!

1. Play as the new hero BoltBreak, who was gifted godlike abilities.

2. Gifted godlike speed, use it to defeat a large onslaught of enemies with superpowers.

3. You may have godlike powers, but so do your enemies.

4. 8 Real-world-inspired arena maps for you to fight in using the abilities of gods.
